Native Plant Sales/Vendors in Northern Virginia

Many vendors sell native plants in the spring and the fall. If you “google” the title of this page, you will find many options for buying native plants. While our intention is not to recommend any vendor, below is a list of common places that sell native plants on dates that vary from year to year in spring and fall:

  • Northern Alexandria Native Plant Sale - St. Clement’s Church, Alexandria, VA
  • Earth Sangha Nursery - Springfield, VA
  • Friends of Riverbend Park - Great Falls, VA
  • Green Springs Garden Center (1 day vendor sale that features both native and non-native vendors – Annandale, VA
  • Long Branch Nature Center (online sale) - Arlington, VA
  • Loudon Wildlife Conservancy - Morven Park, Leesburg, VA
  • Nature By Design - Alexandria, VA
  • NOVA Soil and Water Conservation District, Native Seedling Sale – Online (Annandale)
  • Reston Association (has a booth by Nature by Design)
  • Virginia Native Plant Society – Sale first Wednesday of every month at propagation beds at Green Spring Gardens and large sale on Green Springs Garden Center Sale Day
